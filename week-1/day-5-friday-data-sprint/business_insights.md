## Business Insights

### 1. Total Orders

The dataset contains 20 total orders.

### 2. Completed Revenue

The completed revenue is the total of all completed orders calculated as quantity × price.

### 3. City with Most Orders

Prishtina has the highest number of orders.

### 4. Category with Most Orders

Electronics is the category with the most orders.

### 5. Most Valuable Order

The most valuable order is the order with the highest total amount.

### 6. Orders That Should Not Count as Revenue

Pending and cancelled orders should not be counted as revenue because they are not completed sales.

### 7. One Business Recommendation

Promote the best-selling category and improve sales in cities with fewer orders. Reduce cancelled orders by improving the ordering process.

### 8. What We Would Show to the Business Owner

- Total number of orders
- Completed revenue
- City with the most orders
- Category with the most orders
- Most valuable order
- Order status summary

### 9. What Python Helped Us Understand

Python helped us read the CSV file, clean the data, calculate revenue, count orders by city and category, and identify business trends.

### 10. What SQL Helped Us Understand

SQL helped us filter data, calculate revenue, group orders by city and category, count records, and find the highest-value orders.
