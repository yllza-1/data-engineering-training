# Bronze, Silver, and Gold Data Layers Explanation

## Bronze Layer - Raw Tables

The Bronze layer contains the original data collected from different sources.

The data is stored in its first form without cleaning or changing.

Examples of Bronze tables:

- orders table
- customers table
- products table

The purpose of this layer is to keep the original data and make it available for future processing.

## Silver Layer - Cleaned Tables

The Silver layer contains cleaned and improved data.

In this layer:

- missing values are fixed
- incorrect formats are corrected
- duplicate records are removed
- data values are standardized
- information is prepared for analysis

The purpose of this layer is to create reliable and high-quality data.

## Gold Layer - Business Reporting Outputs

The Gold layer contains final reports used by businesses for analysis and decision making.

Examples:

- completed revenue reports
- sales performance reports
- product analysis reports
- city performance reports

The Gold layer transforms cleaned data into useful business information.
